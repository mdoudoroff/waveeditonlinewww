WAVEEDIT ONLINE

WaveEdit is the free, open-source wavetable editor developed by Synthesis Technology for the E370 Quad Morphing VCO and E352 Cloud Terrarium VCO Eurorack format wavetable oscillators.

You can download WaveEdit at http://synthtech.com/waveedit for Mac, Windows and Linux.
