WAVEEDIT ONLINE

WaveEdit is the free, open-source wavetable editor developed by Synthesis Technology for the E370 Quad Morphing VCO and E352 Cloud Terrarium VCO Eurorack format wavetable oscillators. You can download WaveEdit at http://synthtech.com/waveedit for Mac, Windows and Linux.

This site presents a growing library of free wavetable banks shared by WaveEdit users via the WaveEdit Online tab within the software. All wavetables here are under CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0) Public Domain Dedication.
