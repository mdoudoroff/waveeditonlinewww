WAVEEDIT ONLINE

WaveEdit is the free, open-source wavetable editor developed by Synthesis Technology for the E370 and E352 Eurorack wavetable oscillators. Details: http://synthtech.com/waveedit

Below is a “picture gallery” of wavetables you can freely download from within the WaveEdit software. There, you can manipulate these wavetables and export them for use with your Synthesis Technology oscillator. Moreover, you can also create your own wavetables and upload them for others to enjoy.
